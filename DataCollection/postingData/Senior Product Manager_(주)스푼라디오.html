기업 이미지

기업 소개

Spoon is the hottest media startup to come out of Asia since TikTok. We reimagine how people express themselves and connect with each other through audio, which is the most visceral medium. Imagine blog to Twitter, Youtube to TikTok, and Podcast to Spoon. We strive for turning users into fans while having fun doing it.
We are looking for a strong, talented senior product manager who will set the product for success as we expand from audio live streaming to a social media platform. If you are interested in a truly global role, leading a platform that operates in Korea, Japan, MENA, the US, and beyond, read on!

주요 업무

• Lead the new product initiative and work with engineering including Machine Learning team, researchers, designers, the program management team and cross-functional teams such as marketing and business group.

• Innovate product offerings by turning information into contextual insights working with researchers, community managers and top-class advisors in Seoul, Japan and San Francisco.

• Participate as a product leader in the product strategy discussion, while conveying the direction to the organization and communicating the value of the work that the team is doing.

• Suggest improvement ideas for our product planning and execution process.

• Participate in all aspects of the development lifecycle, from definition through deployment and support.

• Own product KPIs and make a plan to improve them.

• Analyze and report on app feature performance from high-level KPIs to deep-dives into systems and mechanics.

• Coordinate closely with the Korea-based engineering team to ensure that requirements are clearly defined and implemented correctly.

자격 요건

• 5+ years of product management experience ideally in consumer social and mobile business

• You are an expert in leading the team to craft problem statements and validate hypotheses instead of releasing features.

• You are detail-oriented and understand how to design cohesive user experience to engage users as well as to meet the business goal.

• You are inspiring and charismatic while managing to be humble and open to feedback.

• You are a planner and a communicator and excel at balancing the product goals, the customer as well as cross-functional teams' needs.

• You have experience running hyper scale consumer products, ideally in social and entertainment.

• You utilize data, competitive research, and user feedback to define problems, and formulate solutions and ideas to improve on key metrics.

• You have strong quantitative and analytical skills with the ability to set clear milestones and objectives.

• Experience communicating product roadmaps and market analysis to Head of Product

• You are fluent in Japanese and/or English to understand customers in global markets.

우대사항

• Have experience with various data analysis tools including Amplitude, Tableau, Looker, Appsflyer, etc.

• Have experience running SQL queries and distilling analyses into actionable insights.

• Are willing to spend time in Tokyo & San Francisco in order to work more efficiently with the teams there.

• Fluency in Korean & Japanese and/or English will be extremely useful in this role.

개인정보 수집 및 이용 안내

지원자께서 스푼라디오 입사지원을 위해 제공한 개인정보의 이용 목적, 항목, 보관기간에 대한 안내입니다.

1. 개인정보의 수집ㆍ이용 목적 : 본인 확인, 입사 전형 진행, 합격 여부 확인, 입사 지원자와의 원활한 의사소통, 지원내역 확인, 상시채용

2. 수집하려는 개인정보의 항목 : 성명, 연락처, 이메일, 경력사항, 지원 양식에 따른 입사서류

3. 입사 지원을 위해 제공한 개인정보는 1년동안 보관 후 파기 됩니다. 단, 입사지원자가 개인정보의 삭제를 요청하는 경우 지체 없이 이를 삭제하겠습니다.

4. 입사지원자는 개인정보 수집 및 이용 동의를 거부할 수 있으나, 이 경우 입사 전형 관련 일정이 진행될 수 없다는 점 양해 부탁드립니다.
위 내용에 대해 충분히 읽고 이해하신 경우 “지원하기”를 통해 개인정보를 제공에 동의해 주시기 바랍니다.
