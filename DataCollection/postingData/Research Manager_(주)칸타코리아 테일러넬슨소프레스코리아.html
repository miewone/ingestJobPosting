기업 소개

칸타 코리아는 국내 No.1 마케팅리서치 기업으로 데이터를 기반으로 기업경영·마케팅 의사결정과 공공기관·정부의 여론수렴을 지원하는 글로벌 시장조사 및 여론조사 기업입니다.

3만여 명이 90개 국가에서 활약하고 있는 세계 정상의 데이터·인사이트·컨설팅 기업인 칸타의 한국지사입니다.
1984년 프랭크 스몰 앤 어소시에이츠(Frank Small & Associates) 한국지사 설립으로 국내 시장에서 마케팅 조사를 시작한 이래, 오늘날 회사 규모와 매출 기준으로 국내 시장·여론조사 산업의 독보적인 선두기업으로 성장했습니다.

마케팅조사 사업부문은 칸타 코리아의 핵심 사업부문으로서 한국과 세계 시장에서 성장을 추구하는 고객사에 심층적인 맞춤형 시장조사를 제공합니다. 업계 최대 규모의 컨설팅 인원과 오퍼레이션 조직을 운용하고 있습니다.

업계 1위 리서치사 칸타코리아의 Research Manager 포지션 채용 중입니다.
리서치 경력자 분들의 많은 지원 부탁드립니다!

주요 업무

Client Relationship
- Manage the day-to-day relationship with key contact (Maintain and grow a network of contacts at the client site)
- Maintain and develop smooth relationships with the Clients and manage proactively and sensitively these relationships when difficult circumstances might occur
- Express outward confidence in the relationship with clients which will help in facilitating problem solution and issues to be overcome in a confident manner
- Manage the practical/on the ground management of the delivery of the contracted service with respect to quality, speed and volume of data and the servicing they are entitled to
- Conduct account planning and manage workload issues and keep lines of communication with client open and communicate upwards

Account Handling
- Where appropriate have some management responsibility for account administration, coordinating upwards to Directors and appropriate information to accounts and delivery service departments
- Ensures all expiring contracts are renewed beforehand
- Be aware of contractual obligations and detail and manage any potential conflicts around this subject upwards in good time
- Proactively manage the communication pathway to senior support ensuring that they are kept up to date with client issues and developments Work with senior support to develop and execute account plans (e.g. additional analyses and hence develop potential revenue opportunities)

Service Development
- Continually seek out opportunities to bring to the client’s attention developments to the service and potentially new ways of working


Basic Data & Tracking
- Manage the reporting process to clients
- Proactively identify opportunities to evolve our deliverables as new technology or tools become available and/or as client businesses change focus or structure

Analysis & Insight
- Demonstrating insight to clients – with clear, simple and direct messages on implications and recommendations
- Taking briefs from clients, and manage the production of appropriate materials/presentations/written documentation in response to brief
- Full Powerview knowledge of basic and special analysis
- Deliver presentations with recommendations that actively seek to meet the objectives of the client’s brief
- Liaise with other Worldpanel service teams/Custom (as appropriate) in order to deliver insightful work to the client
