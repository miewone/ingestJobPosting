주요 업무

[ About the role ]
MOLOCO seeks an exceptional Technical Account Manager(TAM) to join us in supporting a state-of-the-art advertising platform. You will be part of the Retail Media Platform(RMP) Customer Engineering team supporting the digital marketplace customers using RMP.

The TAM will be the trusted advisor and designated technical point of contact for the RMP customers. The TAM is an essential role to enhance customer experience of RMP and bridge customer business with MOLOCO RMP product and engineering.

As a company founded by Software Engineers, we understand the value of a strong engineering team and strive to hire only the best talent. While tackling challenging real-world problems, you will make a positive impact on leading digital marketplaces and their users in the world and grow with top-notch colleagues.

[ What you'll do ]
• Provide technical guide and suggest RMP best practices for the customers.
• Take a lead on customer-related activities and processes such as incident management and response, planned maintenance, and feature release processes.
• Co-work with the Customer Solutions Engineers(CSE) on developing technical assets for customers.
• Proactively troubleshoot technical errors and issues, and resolve or escalate internally within the team.
• Analyze customer data and integration work to identify and mitigate data quality issues.

자격 요건

• 5+ years of work experience, with at least 3+ years of customer-facing solutions/integration engineering roles
• Computer science, or equivalent technical background
• Good understanding of online service systems and businesses; especially SaaS industries and API-based technologies.
• Data-driven and analytical mindset
• Fast learner and self-initiative personality
• Comfortable with a fast-paced environment
• Fluency both in Korean and English
