기업 소개

About StradVision:
StradVision is an industry pioneer in vision processing technology, accelerating the advent of fully autonomous vehicles. StradVision's SVNet is being deployed on 50+ vehicle models in partnership with 13 OEMs worldwide, and is serviced by over 250 employees in Seoul, San Jose, Detroit, Tokyo, Munich, Dusseldorf and Shanghai. As we are focused on securing differentiated technologies, we are actively conducting research and development with engineering experts, who make up than 85% of all employees. We have acquired 190 + patents and filed more than 700+ patents in Korea, the United States, Europe, and Japan.

StradVision has been honored with the Gold Award at the 2021 AutoSens Awards for Best-in-Class Software for Perception Systems, and the 2020 Autonomous Vehicle Technology ACES Award in Autonomy (Software Category). In addition, StradVision's software is certified to the ISO 9001:2015 international standard.

주요 업무

Mission of the Role:
As Software Process Engineer you are responsible to develop and continuously improve standard development process. Furthermore, you will have the opportunity to guide engineers within our software organization to achieve a high standard for software development process application in compliance with industry standards. You collaborate with the process owners and software engineering experts to develop and implement software engineering best practices. You mentor individual associates and serve as primary contact for software engineering best practices.

Key Responsibilities:
• Develop and improve company standard development process in compliance with ASPICE, ISO26262, SOTIF and Cybersecurity
• Analyze team specific process landscape and work with the development teams to achieve compliance to ASPICE, ISO26262 and ISO21434
• Coach development teams on the implementation of ASPICE, Functional Safety (ISO26262), SOTIF, Cybersecurity (ISO21434) for SOP projects
• Support development team to create software artifacts and documentations, if necessary, participate in reviews
• Work with the project and quality managers to ensure process adherence
